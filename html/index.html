Instructions

The game is split into a series of categories with a fixed number of puzzles per category. Each category has a clue, which is shown near the top of the page. Underneath this is a series of consonants and spaces, and the goal is to reinsert the vowels to find the answer. The spaces are not in the correct place.

For example, if the category clue was "Cocktails" and the consonants were "WHTR SSN" the answer would be "White Russian".

The case of the letters doesn't matter, and apart from the spaces, all punctuation is ignored. You get 10 seconds per puzzle, and that includes typing time.

Press space or enter to start. Press enter to guess a word. Press escape to stop the game.
